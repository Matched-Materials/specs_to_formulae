For a formulation with ~11.4% CaCO3 filler, a modulus in the 1.0-1.4 GPa range would be more typical, even accounting for the elastomer. This suggests the model may be inaccurate in predicting stiffness.
*   **Other Properties (Plausible):** MFI, yield strength, HDT, and density are all within reasonable ranges for this type of compound. The low HDT is consistent with the absence of a nucleator or reinforcing filler like talc.
